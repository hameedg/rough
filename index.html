DETAILED ITENERARY

DAY - 1

Dehradun - Pantwari - Kathian Base Camp

80km drive(3 hours) & 6km trek(4 hours)

The journey will start after a small safety briefing and will take you approximately 4 hrs to reach Kathian Meadow (base camp for the night) through the beautiful jungle of cedar and rhododendron. Enjoy the evening bonfire, with soup and popcorn and spend cosy night at the base camp, Kathian Meadows.


DAY - 2

Kathian - Nagtibba Summit - Dehradun

80km drive(3 hours) & 16km trek(8 hours)

Good Morning,feel the welcoming morning mountain sun!
After a delicious meal, we will start to step down towards Pantwari and reach there by 4:00 p.m.
Depart from Pantwari - Dehradun with memories to cherish for! Reach Dehradun by 7 p.m.
